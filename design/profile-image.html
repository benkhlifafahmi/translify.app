2

Saves a 400×400 PNG to Downloads.

OR — headless one-liner (requires Chrome + ImageMagick):

google-chrome --headless --screenshot=/tmp/lumi.png --window-size=400,400 --hide-scrollbars "file://$(pwd)/profile-image.html" && convert /tmp/lumi.png -gravity North -crop 400x400+0+0 +repage profile-export.png
