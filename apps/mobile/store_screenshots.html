Le Petit Prince
What does "L'essentiel est invisible" actually mean?
🦉
It's the book's most famous line — what truly matters can't be seen with eyes, only felt with the heart. The Fox teaches this to the Little Prince before their goodbye.
Is "essentiel" related to essence?
🦉
Exactly! Both come from Latin essentia → "being." In French, essentiel means what's at the core of something's existence.
